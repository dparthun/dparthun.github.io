The primary message of this narrative visualization is to highlight the impact of Covid-19 on colleges and prisons across five major US states (California, Florida, Illinois, New York, and Texas), showcasing differences in case counts relative to the populations of these institutions.

For this project, I built a martini glass narrative visualization using Covid data. The martini glass structure starts with a broad overview and then narrows down to specific details, providing opportunities for the viewer to drill down into the data. The structure ensures that users first understand the context before exploring the data in depth.
